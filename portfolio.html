My Work

I am a cinematographer, a storyteller through the lens. I capture fleeting moments and craft them into visual poetry, where light, shadow, and movement come together to evoke emotions. Through my camera, I turn the ordinary into something extraordinary, using every frame to speak where words fall short. My work is a blend of technique and artistry, constantly pushing boundaries to create stories that resonate long after the screen fades to black.

Professional Background

A seasoned cinematographer with over a decade of experience in visual storytelling, I have worked across a wide range of genres, from intimate dramas to expansive action films. With a background in both film studies and hands-on production, I began my career in independent cinema, where I honed my skills in natural lighting, composition, and the art of capturing emotion through the lens. My journey has taken me from set to set, collaborating with directors, producers, and actors to bring stories to life with a unique visual style that emphasizes both the grand and the subtle moments. I have worked internationally, adapting to diverse landscapes and cultural narratives, which has expanded my ability to create immersive worlds through camera work. Whether shooting on film or digital, I am committed to pushing the limits of what is possible, blending tradition with innovation to create films that speak to the audience on a deeply emotional level

Professional Experience

Cinematographer | Freelance
January 2015 – Present
As a freelance cinematographer, I’ve had the opportunity to work on a diverse range of projects, from feature films and commercials to music videos and documentaries. My role involves collaborating with directors and producers to craft visually compelling narratives that align with their vision. Whether working on a low-budget indie film or a high-end commercial, I bring my technical expertise and creative eye to each project, ensuring the cinematography supports and enhances the storytelling.

“City Streets” (Documentary, 2022) – Captured raw, documentary-style footage to tell the story of urban life in a global city. Worked with minimal crew and lighting to achieve an authentic, immersive experience.
“Waves of Time” (Short Film, 2021) – Used a combination of handheld and steady cam shots to create a dynamic, emotional connection between the audience and the characters in this experimental drama.
